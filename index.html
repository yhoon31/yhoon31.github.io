Yonghoon Lee

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Statistics at The University of Pennsylvania, advised by Professor Edgar Dobriban and Professor Eric J. Tchetgen Tchetgen. I am interested broadly in developing theories and methodologies for statistical inference, and my current research interests include distribution-free inference, multiple hypotheses testing, and causal inference. Previously, I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Rina Foygel Barber during my Ph.D. studies in statistics at The University of Chicago. Prior to UChicago, I received my Bachelor's degree in mathematics and statistics from Seoul National University.
